Introducing a New Line of Hardened, Reusable, ‘Duplex’ Screws to the Form Setting Industry
All our fasteners are purpose-made, to do one job and do it perfectly.

They aren’t 'Screws' in the traditional sense because they are hardened and made to be used again and again, dropping COST PER USE to near zero.

Leading With, the 'Big Johnson'
Standing at an overall length of 3-3/4" (including the 3/4" 'duplex' head), with the shank's overall length of 2-7/8", it is perfectly suited for 2x to 2x connections.
Up Next is the Johnson 'Bridge Buddy'
A hefty boy made for heavy timber set-up. Like all Johnson Tools (they look like screws, but they ain't), the Bridge Buddy will be made to do one job and do it right.
Coming Soon, the 'Little Johnson'
Made for steel stake to LVL (Laminated Veneer Lumber) form connections, will sport the same High Hat ('Duplex') square-drive head for easy location and removal post-pour.
